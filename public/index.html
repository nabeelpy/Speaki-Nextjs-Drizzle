Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning English with FreeSpeaki.

Is FreeSpeaki free to use?

Yes! Our Free plan gives you access to foundational lessons and community forums — no credit card required. When you're ready for more, upgrade to Pro for unlimited courses, live tutoring, and advanced AI feedback.

What English levels do you support?

We offer structured courses from complete beginner (A1) up through advanced (C2), plus specialized business English. When you sign up, our placement test evaluates your current level and recommends the perfect starting point.

How does the AI pronunciation feedback work?

Our AI listens as you speak and provides instant, word-by-word feedback on your pronunciation, intonation, and fluency. It highlights specific areas for improvement with visual guides and tracks your accuracy over time so you can see measurable progress.

Can I learn English on my phone?

Absolutely. FreeSpeaki is fully responsive and works beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. All your lessons and progress sync across devices, so you can practice on your commute and continue at home without missing a beat.

Do you offer live tutoring sessions?

Yes — Pro and Enterprise members get access to live 1-on-1 sessions with certified English tutors. Sessions are 25 or 50 minutes and can be scheduled at your convenience, with availability across all time zones.
